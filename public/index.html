How to use
Step-by-step
Start your first call
  • Open this page on two devices or tabs.
  • On the device with a camera (phone/laptop), click Start Camera.
  • Then, on either device, click Start Call to connect.
  • If a device has no mic/camera (like your PC), it can still join as receive-only.

This demo uses WebRTC for peer-to-peer media and Socket.IO just for signaling (offer / answer / ICE candidates).
